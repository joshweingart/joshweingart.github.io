Fitch Ratings is an American credit rating agency. It is one of the "Big Three credit rating agencies", along with Moody's and Standard & Poor's.

At Fitch, I work with a global design team to contribute to the branding and design strategy for their marketing and analytical teams. I also translate complex financial data into compelling visuals across print, digital, and interactive platforms. In addition, I ensure brand consistency in all internal and external design materials. I’ve created brochures, animated ads, social media, presentations, websites, and more.
