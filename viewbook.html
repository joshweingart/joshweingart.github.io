The School of Visual Arts Digital Viewbook is a parallax website that uses a wide array of scroll effects to entice prospective undergrad SVA students. I used Sketch and Principle to create mobile and desktop interactive prototypes and to simulate parallax scrolling effects. I then handed off designs and prototypes to developers in GitLab.

The project was a 2021 Webby People's Voice Winner for best school/university website!

As part of the SVA’s in-house design studio, the Visual Arts Press, I worked with Brian Smith, Eric Corriel, and Declan Van Welie.
