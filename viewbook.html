The School of Visual Arts Digital Viewbook is a promotional website made for SVA. The site uses a wide array of scroll effects to entice prospective undergrad SVA students. I used Sketch, Principle, and Gitlab to create mobile and desktop prototypes of the viewbook, which I then handed off to developers.
